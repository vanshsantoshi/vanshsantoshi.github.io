Android Security & Reverse Engineering Projects

Android Spyware & Keylogging Research

Conducted hands-on research into Android spyware techniques by building controlled proof-of-concept applications. Analyzed misuse of Accessibility Services to capture keystrokes, on-screen text, notifications, and app data. Studied permission abuse, background execution limits, and Play Protect detection behavior on modern Android versions.

Smali-Level Application Instrumentation

Performed Smali-level reverse engineering and bytecode modification on closed-source Android applications. Decompiled APKs, modified manifests, injected custom logic, added permissions, and rebuilt apps without access to source code. Used the workflow to understand real-world app protections and demonstrate attack surfaces in popular applications.

Android App Protection Bypass & Analysis

Analyzed anti-tampering and protection mechanisms including root detection, SSL pinning, and runtime checks. Used tools such as Frida, Jadx, APKTool, and dynamic debugging to bypass protections and observe application behavior at runtime for security research purposes.

Custom Android Input & Automation Research

Designed a custom Android keyboard application to study input handling and UI automation capabilities. Explored interaction between input methods and system services to better understand abuse scenarios and defensive controls. Project maintained as a private research repository.
